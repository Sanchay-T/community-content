## **Event Overview**
[Gemma 2 AI Challenge](https://lablab.ai/event/gemma-2-ai-challenge) brought together a vibrant community of 1,377 participants across 107 teams, united in their passion for advancing AI technology. Over the course of the challenge, these innovators developed 35 groundbreaking AI applications, showcasing the versatility and power of AI in addressing diverse challenges.

The event kicked off on November 28th at the [Google Cloud Space in London](https://maps.app.goo.gl/CsQg6rTvAY9By4Z37), where attendees engaged in hands-on workshops and insightful sessions. Luiz Gustavo Martins (Gus), a Staff Developer Relations Engineer at Google, led the standout workshop, "From Zero to Hero with Gemma Models," providing a deep dive into the capabilities and applications of Google's cutting-edge Gemma Model Family. While part of the audience participated on-site, many others tuned in via a live stream, emphasizing the event‚Äôs hybrid nature.

Powered by Gemma Models, an open suite of high-performance AI tools designed for natural language processing, data handling, coding assistance, and multimodal tasks, participants harnessed lightweight and efficient frameworks to create solutions across various domains. With much of the hackathon taking place online, teams leveraged these advanced tools to collaboratively push the boundaries of AI innovation.

## **üåü Hackathon Challenge**
The Gemma 2 AI Challenge invited participants to harness the advanced capabilities of Gemma 2 models to create AI solutions addressing real-world problems or enhancing processes. Teams could innovate by streamlining workflows, automating tasks, or improving decision-making across industries. They were also encouraged to transform communication through enhanced language understanding and natural conversations, or develop inclusive, scalable tools that worked across languages and cultures. Additionally, participants could build AI-powered tools to boost productivity, creativity, or development in areas like coding and data analysis. The challenge showcased the versatility of Gemma 2 in solving real-world issues and driving innovation.

The Granite AI Models family, developed by IBM, encompasses a range of state-of-the-art language models tailored for various applications. These models provide robust solutions for creating interactive chatbots, executing precise instructions and supporting multiple languages. Each model in the Granite AI Models family is designed to deliver high performance and accuracy, making them ideal for innovative and complex language processing tasks.

Granite AI Models are [available on Hugging Face under the Apache 2.0 license](https://huggingface.co/ibm-granite). 


## Granite AI Models

**Granite 13b Chat:** This model is designed for creating interactive chatbot applications that can understand and generate human-like responses, enhancing user engagement and support capabilities.
---
**Granite 13b Instruct:** Optimized for following detailed instructions, this model excels at tasks requiring precise adherence to user commands, making it ideal for complex workflow automation and support tasks.
---
**Granite Multilingual:** A powerful model supporting multiple languages, Granite Multilingual is suitable for applications requiring understanding and generation of text in various languages, broadening accessibility and reach.
---
**Granite Japanese:** Specialized in Japanese language processing, this model is tailored for applications needing high proficiency in understanding and generating Japanese text, from chatbots to content creation tools.
